Focaccia with Caramelized Onions, Cramberries, and Brie

Wow your taste buds with this festive focaccia appetizer topped with sweet caramellized onions, tart cramberries, and creamy brie. It will become your new favorite holiday starter!

Ingredients

    For the focaccia:
  • 4 cups (500g) all purpose flour
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons instant yeast
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons salt
  • 1 3/4 cups (350) lukewarm water
  • 1 1/3 cups extra virgin olive oil, plus more for the pan
